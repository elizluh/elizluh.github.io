Elizabeth Luh

University of Houston

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the University of Houston. I will be joining the Criminal Justice Administrative Records System (CJARS) at the University of Michigan as a post-doc in the summer. I am primarily interested in Applied Microeconomics with a focus on labor economics, public economics, and economics of crime. My current research is focused on identifying and measuring racial bias in law enforcement.
